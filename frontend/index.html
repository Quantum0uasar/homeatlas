Mortgage Process

  • Pre-approval: Build the buyer profile and understand budget before committing to a home.
  • Property selection: Compare homes, pricing, and monthly carrying cost.
  • Offer stage: Work with the realtor to submit an offer and negotiate terms.
  • Financing review: Confirm mortgage details, supporting documents, and final affordability.
  • Closing: Prepare cash to close, sign documents, and complete the purchase.

Typical Documents Needed

  • Government photo ID
  • Proof of income
  • Employment confirmation
  • Proof of down payment
  • Recent bank statements
  • Accepted purchase agreement
